What factors should they be scored on?

Scoring is always on a three-point scale, from 1 (least) to 3 (most).

Much of the time, the best possible score for a factor will be 3.

However, sometimes you will want a high score in a particular factor to be bad. The best score for this kind of factor is not 3, but 1. One example of this might be 'difficulty', where an option which has high difficulty is less good than one with low difficulty.
